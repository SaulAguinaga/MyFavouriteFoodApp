TORTILLA DE PATATA

    INGREDIENTS

  • 3 Large Potatoes
  • 6 Eggs
  • 1 Sweet Onion
  • Salt
  • Oil

TIME

Preparation

10min

Cooking

20min

    PREPARATION

  1. Peel the potatoes and cut them into small squares.
  2. Peel the onion and cut it into very small pieces.
  3. Fry the potatoes and the onion in separate frying pans over medium heat in oil.
  4. When the potatoes and onion are poached, add the eggs and salt to a bowl and mix with the potatoes and onions.
  5. In a frying pan, over medium high heat, pour the misture and let it brown on the outside of the pan.
  6. The "tortilla" can be made to taste. The eggs can be undercooked or overcooked.
